Lexi's Amazing Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bag of microwave popcorn, preferably buttered
  • 2 tbsp Sriracha
  • Optional: 1 tbsp lime juice

Directions

  1. Put the popcorn in the microwave, follow instructions on bag.
  2. Take popcorn out of the microwave, and put it in a bowl. Make sure you don't burn yourself!
  3. Add Sriracha.
  4. Add limejuice if so desired.
  5. Shake it up.
  6. Eat!
